Our Story

More Than a Shop — A Trusted Partner in Your Child's Education

Our Promise to Every Family

Established in 2010, Tanwer Books & Uniforms has grown to become the most trusted educational supplies shop in the Yelahanka–Dwarka Nagar area. We understand that buying books and uniforms for the new school year can be stressful — we make it effortless, affordable, and even enjoyable.

Run by the Tanwer family with genuine care and warmth, we serve students from Nursery to Class 12 across CBSE, ICSE, and Karnataka State Boards. Just walk in, tell us your child's school and class, and we'll handle everything.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what parents ask us the most.

Do you stock books for all schools in Yelahanka?

Yes! We stock books for virtually all schools in the Yelahanka, Dwarka Nagar, Kattigenahalli, and surrounding areas — including CBSE, ICSE and Karnataka State Board. Just walk in with your school name and class; we'll guide you through everything.

Do you sell second-hand / used textbooks?

Yes, we have a selection of good-condition second-hand textbooks at up to 50% off the regular price. This is a popular option for budget-conscious families. Ask our staff for availability when you visit, or WhatsApp us your book list first.

What are your store timings?

We are open Monday to Saturday, approximately 9:00 AM – 7:30 PM, and Sundays 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM. Hours may be extended during peak school season (June–July). Call us at 09916049273 to confirm current timings.

Can I WhatsApp my book list before visiting?

Absolutely! WhatsApp us your book list at 09916049273 before you visit and we'll check availability and keep it ready. This saves you time, especially during the busy school season rush in June–July.

Do you have school uniforms in all sizes?

Yes, we stock uniforms in a wide range of sizes for boys and girls from Nursery to Class 12 — shirts, trousers, skirts, ties, belts, socks, and PE kits. Come in with your child for the best fit.

Where exactly is the shop located?

Shop No 102, Vinayaka Complex, Bagalur Main Road, Dwarka Nagar (near Vinayak Nagar), Yelahanka, Bengaluru – 560063. We are opposite The Big Market. Use Google Maps for easy navigation — link is in the Contact section below.

Is there parking available near the store?

Yes, there is street parking available on Bagalur Main Road near the Vinayaka Complex. The area is easily accessible by auto, bus, and private vehicle. Bus stop is very close to the shop.
